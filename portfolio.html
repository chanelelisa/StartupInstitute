The Startup Institute Web Design Track is a highly selective and immersive program designed to cover every phase of front-end development and interface design with an emphasis on user-centered principles. Built on the product development life cycle, the curriculum simulates real-life challenges to prepare you to successfully take a project from concept to launch. The Design program intensely covers universal principals, user-personas, low-fidelity mockups, wireframing, rapid prototyping, fonts & layouts, navigation flow, product launch and project management. The industry languages HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery are learned at a highly functional and comprehensive level.
