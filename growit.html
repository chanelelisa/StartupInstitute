Growit! Mobile App

GrowIt! Is the fastest growing digital community for people who like plants. The platform is entirely mobile and available for Free on the App Store and Google Play. Growit! makes it easy for people to find great plants that they might be interested in and learn how to grow them. They can also find others in their area who have the same type of plant interests. The app uses your gps to show you plants and people around you so that the content is always relevant.

The Project Summary

As the project manager, I led a group of 3 developers in the redesign of the Growit app to include a more interconnected way for the users to communicate. We developed new features like a chat page where the users could up-vote/down-vote answers to questions. We encouraged the users to interact by initiating a gaming/reward aspect on their personal icon. This gives both recognition and validity to the user and is a way for the user to establish themselves in the Growit community.

The Challanges

  • Users want to be able to ask general questions not related to a photo.
  • Users want another way to communicate other than photo comments.

The Goals

  • Identify a solution to the two challenges.
  • Make it easier for users to find the information they are looking for.
  • Give users reasons to come back to the app more often.
  • Make a prototype.

Deliverables

  • Research report
  • Solution proposal
  • Wire frames
  • Simulated demo / functioning app

Communication

  • Held weekly meetings between our team and our partners to check-in with them and collaborate on their ideas.
  • Meetings determined our project direction.
  • Our team met each morning to work on our deliverables, compare research and go over wireframes/prototypes.

Research

  • Comparative apps, specifically the way that their users communicated with each other.
  • Optimizing user interactiveness.
  • Button placements, scroll features, navigation bar placements.
  • Referred to the data on the Nielson Norman Group Reports.

Execution

  • Wireframes: Low-fidelity mock-ups.
  • Design Layout: Balsamiq, Sketch and Photoshop (high-fidelity).
  • Prototype: Invision software. The prototype app navigated through the new functions clearly with optimal usability.
